Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership

The NP (Non-Profit) Membership is tailored specifically for non-profit organizations looking to enhance their community presence and network with local businesses.

This membership level provides access to resources and support that help non-profits thrive, including exclusive networking events, promotional opportunities, and educational workshops aimed at non-profit success.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership offers a solid foundation for small businesses or entrepreneurs seeking to establish themselves in the community.

Benefits include a basic listing in the chamber's online directory, access to business resources, invitations to member-only events, and the opportunity to participate in chamber committees.

This membership level is perfect for businesses looking to grow their network and visibility.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership is designed for businesses that are ready to take their engagement with the chamber to the next level.

In addition to all the benefits of the Bronze Membership, Silver members receive enhanced directory listings, opportunities for event sponsorship, and greater visibility through chamber marketing channels.

This level provides a balanced mix of networking, marketing, and professional development benefits.

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership represents the premier level of engagement with the chamber of commerce, ideal for businesses seeking maximum exposure and influence in the community.

Gold members enjoy all the benefits of the Silver Membership, plus premium directory listings, VIP access to major events, exclusive advertising opportunities, and strategic introductions to key community and business leaders.

This top-tier membership is perfect for established businesses looking to solidify their leadership role within the local economy.
